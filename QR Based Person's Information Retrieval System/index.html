ABOUT US


According to a report by World Bank, “There are 53 road accidents every hour and 1 death every four minutes in India”. In most of those cases, victim don't have anyone known along with them. In such situation, the family of the victim is also being notified only after the completion of identificaton process which sometimes takes a lot more time depending on the seriousness of the victim's injuries. E-Tag offers you and your loved once a helping hand during such emergencies.

E-Tag has been using best technologies that ensures speedy activity during emergencies. To find solution to your problem is our main goal. Whereas technologies such as QR Code based Information Retrieval, GPS Tracking and Location sharing, etc. are our handy tools to acieve it.
